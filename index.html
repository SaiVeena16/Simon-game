Hi! Welcome to the Simon Challenge Game

  1. The goal of the game is to remember and repeat a sequence of flashing buttons or colors.
  2. The game starts with a single button or color flashing.
  3. The player must click on the same button or color to proceed to the next level.
  4. If the player clicks the wrong button or color, the game restarts from the beginning.
  5. Each level adds one more button or color to the sequence.
  6. The player needs to remember the entire sequence from previous levels to succeed.
  7. The player wins by correctly repeating the entire sequence up to the highest level reached.
